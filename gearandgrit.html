How I Work With Brands

VOLUME IS WHERE
THE MAGIC HAPPENS.

I'll be straight with you: 1–3 videos won't move the needle. Here's why I only take on brands ready to actually win.

1–3 Videos

Most creators will take your money, post a few videos, and call it done. But TikTok doesn't work that way — and neither do I.

  • Not enough data to know what's working
  • One angle, one shot — no room to iterate
  • Algorithm barely registers you as a signal
  • No time to find the hook that goes viral
  • Brands walk away thinking "TikTok doesn't work"

10–20 Videos

This is where brands actually break through. More volume means more angles, more hooks, more chances to find the one that pops — and when it does, everything else amplifies.

  • Test multiple hooks until one cracks
  • Iterate on what the data tells us
  • Build real signal with the TikTok algorithm
  • Diverse angles: demo, lifestyle, proof, story
  • One viral video can pay for the entire package
  • Compound effect — videos keep working over time
